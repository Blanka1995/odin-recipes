How to Make Vegetable Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Fresh Vegies
  • Lasagna sheets
  • Ricotta
  • Spinach
  • Pecorino and mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil or Parsley

Steps

  1. Roast the veggies with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  2. Boil the lasagna sheets for couple of minutes unless they don't need boiling as some sheets come pre-boiled. Toss them with a drizzle of olive oil to prevent sticking
  3. Make the ricotta filling. Mix the ricotta cheese with the lemon zest, garlic, salt, and pepper. Layer the lasagna and bake it for 35 minutes.
